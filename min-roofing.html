Roofing

TITAN Restoration & Construction are experts in the Commercial and Residential Minnesota market.

If your property or business is affected by weather conditions, it is essential to act quickly. It is important to know damage to your roof cannot always be seen from the ground. Your trusted TITAN team provides a thorough inspection of your roof and attic. Let us assist you with the insurance claim process. Our claims specialists will work with your insurance provider and adjuster to expedite the process. We attend the adjuster meetings at your property to assist in documenting damages.

Once your insurance claim is approved, your TITAN representative will assist you in choosing your new roofing materials. Your TITAN representative will stay in constant communication with you during the production process

Upgrading your roof also gives great curb appeal and adds value to your home.

TITAN also offers maintenance programs. We offer several maintenance programs which includes 'at cost' pricing, routine inspections, and maintenance. We offer a labor warranty to ensure complete client satisfaction.

TITAN offers the following roofing systems:

- Tile
- Shingle
- Metal
- Seal Coatings
- Flat
- TPO
- and much more!

TITAN offers Production Specialists on each jobsite for any questions or concerns you may have and to ensure quality workmanship.
